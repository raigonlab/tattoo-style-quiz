## Tips

- Desktop screenshots: use Chrome, zoom at 100%, window ~1280px wide
- Mobile screenshots: Chrome DevTools > Toggle device toolbar > iPhone 12 or similar
- Wireframes: Figma, Balsamiq, or even a clear photo of a hand-drawn sketch works
- All images can be PNG or JPEG
